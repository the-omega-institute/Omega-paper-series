## 🔬 Research Highlights / 研究亮点

### 1. Information Rate Circle (v²_ext + v²_int = c²)
The partition of maximal causal information flow between spatial transport and internal quantum processing.

信息流在空间传输与内部量子处理之间的分配。

### 2. Mass as Topological Impedance
Massive particles emerge as stable feedback loops with impedance matching via Riccati equations.

有质量粒子作为通过Riccati方程进行阻抗匹配的稳定反馈环路涌现。

### 3. Spinor Topology (√Δ → 4π periodicity)
Spin-1/2 arises from the unavoidable square-root discriminant in self-referential dynamics.

自旋-1/2源于自指动力学中不可避免的平方根判别式。

### 4. Einstein Equations from Entropy Stationarity
G_ab + Λg_ab = 8πG T_ab derived from first-order stationarity of generalized entropy.

从广义熵的一阶驻点性推导爱因斯坦方程。

### 5. Gravitational Wave Predictions
Lattice cutoff frequency f_cut ~ c/(πℓ_cell) with Bragg-like oscillations.

晶格截止频率预测。
