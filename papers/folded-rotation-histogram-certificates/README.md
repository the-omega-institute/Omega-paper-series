> **Submitted to:** SIAM Journal on Applied Dynamical Systems

## Key Innovation

A two-stage audit framework for coarse-grained orbit data: stage one certifies whether an empirical folded histogram is close to its deterministic limit (via star-discrepancy, TV, and relative-entropy bounds), stage two tests compatibility with the ambient Parry equilibrium. The Fibonacci weighting is proved uniquely characterized by a contiguous numeration axiom, and the optimal injective placement barrier for lossless coarse-grainings is calibrated exactly.

**Scope:** Applied dynamical systems  
**Keywords:** irrational rotations, Sturmian sequences, Parry measure, Renyi divergence, statistical auditing
